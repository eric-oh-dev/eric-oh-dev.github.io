My Learning Plan

How will I operate within Dev Academy Foundations and Bootcamp Programme.

6 April 2025

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

My goal is to become a web developer. I’m aiming to gain the technical and professional skills needed to work confidently as a junior developer, with a focus on front-end and full-stack development. I want to be part of a team where I can grow, contribute, and eventually mentor others entering the field.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

Strengths: I’m a strong problem-solver who enjoys breaking challenges down into smaller parts and working through them step by step. I also have solid logical thinking skills, which helps me understand how different pieces of code interact and how to structure solutions that make sense.
Limitations: One of my biggest challenges is perfectionism — I often spend too much time trying to get things exactly right, which can slow me down. Hence my second limitation, time management. I especially struggle with time management when balancing multiple tasks or when a project takes longer than expected. I'm working on being more mindful of deadlines and trusting that progress is more important than perfection.

What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

While I'm focused on building my technical skills, I also want to grow in three key human skill areas:
Communication: I want to become more confident in expressing my ideas clearly, asking questions when I need help, and giving and receiving constructive feedback effectively.
Resilience & Growth Mindset: I want to get better at handling challenges without getting discouraged. I’m working on viewing mistakes as learning opportunities rather than setbacks.
Collaboration & Teamwork: I aim to be an open and supportive team member. I want to practice sharing responsibilities, listening actively, and contributing positively to group work.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

To manage my workload during the programme, I’ll set clear goals for myself and stay organized using whatever tools or methods help me stay on track. I’ll make sure to communicate regularly with my peers and facilitators, and contribute positively to group projects by listening actively and being a reliable teammate. When things get overwhelming or stressful, I’ll:
Take short breaks to reset and clear my mind — this might be a quick walk, some deep breathing, or stretching.
Reflect and journal to help process frustration and understand what’s going on internally.
Pair with another learner to talk through problems, both for support and to strengthen understanding.
Ask for help early instead of staying stuck — I’ll reach out to facilitators or peers as soon as I feel blocked.

A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

I hope the facilitation team will be approachable and available when I need help or guidance, and that I’ll feel comfortable reaching out when I’m stuck or unsure. I’d also appreciate emotional support during moments when the workload or content feels overwhelming — even just having someone to talk things through with can make a big difference.
I’m looking to be challenged to grow, think more critically, and push outside of my comfort zone as a learner and as a person. Finally, I’d like support in preparing for life after Dev Academy, including job readiness, what to expect from industry roles, and how to continue growing after the programme.
